Guest-in-town on Wednesday with Hong Phuc from FOSS.Asia

June 24, 2015 at 07:00 PM | categories: event

Hong Phuc (洪福) is our old friend from FOSS.Asia. She had been ever came to our event last year. Now we have a new chance to meet her and talk anything about Free and Open Source Software!
