BLUG Tuesday at the Russian Restaurant (Traktirr Pusksin)

Jun 23 (Tue)
By Admin

In misc.

2015-06-23 07:00:00 PM

Let's meet at Traktirr Pusksin (彼得堡西餐厅) in Dongzhimen this week. It's an old meeting place for BLUG back to 2008, we can share and miss some stories of BLUG's "ancient times".

这周的聚会我们回到彼得堡西餐厅叙叙旧。这里曾经是BLUG在2008年主要的活动场所。我们来回忆一下BLUG的“上古时期” 。

  • Time: 19:00, 2015-06-23 (Tue)
  • Address: 5-15 Dongzhimen Neidajie 东直门内大街5-15
  • Traffic: Subway Line 2, Dongzhimen station, Exit A. Walk to the west for about 300 meters. 地铁二号线A出口向西300米左右.
  • Tel: +86 (010) 8407 8158
