Oct 31 (Sat)

Halloween Hacking, Never stop coding...

Coding-for-fun(乐码周末) is open to anyone interested in sharing and learning with Free Software(自由软件). BLUG membership is not required. Its better to take your laptop!
乐码周末活动面向所有人开放，无论是否为BLUG成员均可参加。最好带上您的笔记本电脑哦!

Special thanks to SUSE Beijing office for the location. 特别感谢 SUSE 北京办公室提供活动场地！

You can do followings here:

  • Coding - of course for any blugers
  • Eating - snack, drink, (Pay yourself for dinner)
  • Watching - documentary movies, on-line videos, we can provide the projector
  • Playing - table games
  • Body Exercise - we have some simple equipments.

您可以在这里：

  • 码 - 垒代码是 Bluger 的主业！
  • 吃 - 零食饮料（晚餐各付各的哦！）
  • 看 - 纪录片，在线视频。我们提供投影机
  • 玩 - 桌游当然有
  • 炼 - 锻炼身体，这里有一些简单的健身器材

Detail:

  • Time: 13:30 - evening
  • Date: Saturday, October 31st, 2015
  • Location: SUSE Beijing Office (Tel 65339000)
  • Address: 北京财富中心3603, Beijing Fortune Center Tower A, 3603
  • Map: OpenStreetMap
  • Subway: Exit D, JinTaiXiZhao station, Line 10 十号线金台夕照站D出口

Tips: Please ask security fo help to open lifts at the first floor. 提示：请在1层大厅找保安协助开电梯。
