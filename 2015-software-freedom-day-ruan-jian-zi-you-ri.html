2015 Software Freedom Day (软件自由日)

软件自由日(Software Freedom Day，SFD)是一个关于自由软件和开源软件的全球性庆祝活动。从2006年开始，定于每年9月的第三个星期六举行。其目的是向公众推广和宣传自由/开源软件。2015 软件自由日清华站将由北京 GNU/Linux 用户组与 清华大学 TUNA 协会携手举办。

Software Freedom Day (SFD) is an annual worldwide celebration of Free Software. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use. SFD 2015 (Tsinghua Station) will be hold together by Beijing GNU/Linux User Group and TsingHua TUNA community.
