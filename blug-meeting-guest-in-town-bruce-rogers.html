BLUG Meeting & Guest in Town - Bruce Rogers

Oct 27 (Tue)
By Admin

In misc.

2015-10-23 06:20:00 PM

Welcome to Bruce Rogers from Provo Utah, who works as a software engineer in SUSE. Since graduating from San Diego State in 1987, Bruce has worked in the computer industry, first doing software testing, then development for NetWare, including kernel development as they Paravirtualized NetWare for running under Xen. For the past 7 years, he has worked on Xen and QEMU. He has 4 children including an adopted chinese daughter.

On the event, Bruce will make a short presentation on virtualization for about 30 minutes. Then have free talks with us on technology and any other topics.
